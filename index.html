About

What is this?

A local reminder app — everything saved in your browser, no accounts needed.

Priority colours

URGENT
MEDIUM
LOW

Red sorts to top, green to bottom. Done items sink — tap ✕ to clear, or tap circle again to restore.

Filtering

  • Filter Out — everything shown, select items to hide.
  • Filter In — nothing shown, select items to show.
  • People and groups work as an AND condition.
  • Tap Filtered ✕ in the header to clear instantly.

Groups

General is always the default. Add custom groups in the add modal — they persist across sessions. Tap the + on any group card to quickly add to that group.

Export / Import

Export saves a .json backup. Import can merge (add new) or overwrite (replace all).
