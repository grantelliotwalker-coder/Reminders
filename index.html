About Reminders

What is this?

A lightweight local reminder app. Everything is saved in your browser — no accounts, no sync, no server.

Colour · Priority

Checking off

  • Tap the circle on a pill to mark it done.
  • Once done, tap the red ✕ that appears to delete it.

Groups & filtering

  • Assign a group when adding a reminder.
  • Use the ☰ button or chips below the header to filter.
  • Your last filter is remembered.

Export / Import

Export saves a .json backup. Import merges by ID — no duplicates.

Keyboard

  • ⌘K / Ctrl+K — open Add
  • Esc — close modal
