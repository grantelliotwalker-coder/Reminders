About

What is this?

A lightweight local reminder app. Everything is saved in your browser — no accounts, no server.

Priority colours

URGENT
MEDIUM
LOW

Red reminders sort to the top, green to the bottom. Within each colour, oldest first.

Checking off

  • Tap the circle to mark done — it fades to the bottom.
  • Tap the red ✕ that appears to delete it.
  • Tap the circle again to move it back into its priority group.

Groups & filtering

  • Assign a group when adding.
  • Use Filter or the chips below the header to filter.
  • Your last filter is remembered.

Export / Import

Export downloads a .json backup. Import merges — no duplicates.
